Halifax

Date: 2023 - 04 - 12

Duration: 4 days

Summary

Your next adventure awaits a stone’s throw from downtown Halifax attractions, restaurants, hotels & the cruise ship terminal. Join us for an authentic East Coast experience on a 2 hour exploratour, a full day or overnight island adventure. Paddle from sunrise to sunset or under the full moon. Discover & explore the second largest natural harbour on the planet & our wild islands. Enjoy marine life encounters, coastal hikes & beautiful beaches, island ecotours, culinary & glamping adventures. To have as much fun as our guides, just remember to pack a sense of adventure & sense of humour!

Sydney

Date: 2023 - 05 - 10

Duration: 3 days

Summary

The best way to experience some of the best coastline in the world? As close to water as possible. Paddle in serene solitude, take a lesson with a pro, or join a guided tour for an intimate exploration of our waters. Get to know Cape Breton Island stroke by stroke with an engaging canoe or kayak expedition. For over 30 years, we have been providing award-winning kayaking experiences around Cape Breton Island. Located on the Cabot Trail, we offer a variety of sea kayaking tours and packages to suit everyone’s needs, from beginner to advanced. We would be delighted to host your tour and share what we know best. Come and see why we received Destination Cape Breton’s 2012 Product Development of the Year Award and TripAdvisor’s Top 10% in the world rating. We were also a finalist for the Tourism Industry Association of Canada’s 2013 Traveller Experience of the Year award.
